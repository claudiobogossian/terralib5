Spatial Analysis Plugin

The emphasis of the spatial analysis is to measure properties and relationships, taking into account the spatial location of the phenomenon under study. The operations available in this plugin are.

  • Bayes Global
  • Bayes Local
  • Geostatistical Methods
  • Kernel Map
  • Kernel Ratio
  • Proximity Matrix Creator
  • Sample Points Generator
  • Skater
  • Spatial Statistics
